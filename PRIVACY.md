# OpenADLC Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-06-28.

## Who we are
StarFang, an AI engineering company, is the controller of the personal data described here.

## What we process, and why
- **Contributors (CLA).** When you sign the Contributor License Agreement through CLA Assistant, we record your name, your email address, your GitHub username, the CLA version, and the signature timestamp. Legal basis: performance of and entry into the CLA with you, and our legitimate interest in keeping a reliable record of contribution rights. We need this record to prove the license chain behind the code, so we keep it for as long as your contribution remains in the project, plus the period during which a legal claim could arise.
- **Issues, pull requests, and discussions.** Anything you post is public and tied to your GitHub account. GitHub is the platform processor; its handling is governed by GitHub's own privacy statement.
- **AUTHORS attribution.** With your contribution we list your name (and optionally a handle) in the `AUTHORS` file, to honor your authorship. You can ask us to adjust how you are credited.

We do not collect special-category data, and we do not sell personal data.

## Who sees it
Your contribution metadata is public by the nature of an open repository. We use GitHub (and CLA Assistant) as processors. Because GitHub operates in the United States, contribution data may be transferred outside the EEA under the appropriate safeguards (for example, Standard Contractual Clauses) that those providers maintain.

## Your rights
Under the GDPR and Romanian data-protection law you may request access to, rectification of, or erasure of your personal data, restrict or object to processing, and request portability. We will honor these, with one limit: we may retain the minimum CLA record needed to keep the license chain valid, because that is necessary for the establishment and defense of legal claims. You can withdraw consent where processing relies on consent, without affecting prior processing. You may complain to a supervisory authority, in Romania the National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP).

## Changes
We will update this notice as the project and the entity evolve, and we will date each change. Material changes will be noted in the changelog.
